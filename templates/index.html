Don't Struggle to Find Things to Do Tonight

Let Us Plan For You

Step 1: Give Us Some Info

Just tell us the size of your group, the time you want to be out, and your budget for the night. If you like a particular part of the city, add that in too!

You can even customize your profile to tell us what types of food you like, your favorite kinds of music, and more, so we can better tailor your plan.

Step 2: Pick an Agenda

We'll provide you with three distinct options for the night. Pick your favorite events and activities from the list. Don't worry, they're all within walking distance of each other.

Select an option and let us take care of the reservations, tickets, and directions. Just get to the subway stop, and the rest is on us.

Step 3: Invite your Friends

Create a Facebook event and invite all your friends so everyone has the details. Simply plan where to meet with your friends, and that's all you need to worry about.

Then simply follow the agenda we have created for you, enjoy your night, and tell us about it when you're done!
